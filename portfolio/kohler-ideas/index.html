The Bold Look of Kohler

An image-centric inspiration blog for Kohler, Wisconsin-based interior manufacturing brand. Features masonry moodboards, articles, and image carousels. I was particularly proud of this site, which was one of my first projects at Tidal where I owned both the front-end and back-end development.

I built out the site on our custom PHP/MongoDB CMS, implemented lightbox and carousel plugins, and wrote custom Javascript to interface with the faceted search navigation.
